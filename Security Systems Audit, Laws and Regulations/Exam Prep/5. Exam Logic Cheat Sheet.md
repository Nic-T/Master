# Exam Logic "Cheat Sheet"

Goal: Memorize these logical triggers for multiple-choice questions.

## 1. Scenarios and Actions

1. ### Scenario: You suspect a crime/fraud.

- **Action:** Report to the Board/Audit Committee. Do not investigate yourself.

2. ### Scenario: You find a small error.

- **Action:** Verify if it is material. Do not report based on hearsay (Inquiry); get Evidence (Inspection).

3. ### Scenario: Resource constraints (time/money).

- **Action:** Perform Risk Assessment $\rightarrow$ Audit High Risk areas.

4. ### Scenario: Management refuses to fix a finding.

- **Action:** If risk is **high**, escalate to the Board. If risk is **low**, document their acceptance.

5. ### Scenario: Conflict between Company Policy and Law.

- **Action:** Follow the Law (Statutory Requirement).
